I am attaching a chapter outline / plan for Chapter [NUMBER]: [TITLE] of my thesis.

Your job is to convert it into a structured JSON for use by an LLM system that
generates writing blueprints (Task.md files) for each subtopic.

The most important field is chapter_arc. This is the argumentative map of the
entire chapter — how all the subtopics connect, build on each other, and together
prove the chapter's goal. Every Task.md generated for any subtopic of this chapter
will be constrained by this arc. It must be specific to THIS chapter, not generic.

Rules:
- chapter_arc: 150–200 words. Describe the argumentative movement, not a table of contents.
  Show HOW the subtopics build on each other. What does Subtopic 1 establish that
  Subtopic 2 depends on? What does the chapter achieve by the final subtopic that
  it could not achieve without the sequence? Be concrete.
- goal: what this chapter must prove for the thesis argument. 2–3 sentences.
- subtopics: extract all subtopics. Number them exactly as in the outline.
- position_in_argument: for each subtopic, one sentence on its specific role within
  the chapter arc. e.g. "Establishes the pre-existing literary norm that subsequent
  subtopics show feminist writers consciously rejected."
- If information is not in the document, use null. Do not invent.

Output ONLY valid JSON. No explanation. No markdown fences.

Required schema:

  "number": 1,
  "title": "Full chapter title",
  "goal": "What this chapter must prove. 2–3 sentences.",
  "chapter_arc": "150–200 words describing the argumentative movement across all subtopics.",

    {
      "number": "1.1",
